swedish_autogen_v4.json and swedish_autogen_c_v4.json are generated by analysis of the spelling/pronounciation of words from the Lexin: Svenskt lexikon list of words available at http://www.sprakochfolkminnen.se/sprak/sprak-och-it/spraklig-infrastruktur/oppna-sprakdata.html. 

Språkrådet is the author of Lexin: Svenskt lexikon.

swedish_sample.json contains entries defined by Max Helmisaari.
swedish_sample.json is not related to nor derivative work of Lexin: Svenskt lexikon.

The dictionaries provided are a work in progress, and any faults and irregularities in the Palantype outline of words are not to be viewed as reflections of the state of Lexin: Svenskt lexikon.
